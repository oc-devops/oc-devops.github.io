UPCOMING & RECENT SESSIONS

WHAT WE'RE ALL ABOUT

Once a Month or More

We meet once a month to share our knowledge and experiences to learn and discuss how to improve collaboration and sharing between teams while leveraging modern tools and high degrees of automation to build a better IT supply chain. We're just getting started, but plan of meetings to last 1-2 hours and to be focused on talks that demonstrate new tools, techniques and the companies that are using them to adopt DevOps.

This is a group targeted to engineers and we welcome both developers and operations folks, tech professionals, managers and team leads, architects and entrepreneurs. We are all coming together to learn about and share information on DevOps processes, tools, practices and their far-reaching benefits.

We welcome everyone and our organizers are friendly people. Feel free to read our Code of Conduct

What is DevOps?

DevOps isn't something that you buy and not someone you can hire. It is a cultural movement; focused on building and operating high-performance organizations based on a set of core principles including sharing, measurement, and automation. If you are interested in learning more about what we are talking about, here are some really useful links to become aquainted with DevOps

  • What is DevOps? In Simple English
  • Infrastructure as Code (IaC) with Martin Fowler
  • Why Are We Racing to DevOps

GET INVOLVED WITH OC DEVOPS

Lead A Session!

Do you have a personal or professional experience that you would like to share? Could be as simple as expereince with a great (or maybe even not so great) tool that helped you?

Reach out to us about the topic you would like to present by emailing our organizers. You can also give feedback on a topic you'd like to see by visiting our Session Ideas wiki.

Show Your Support

Learning and collaborating around DevOps in Orange County, and beyond, is what will make our community grow. Grow your network and meet some smart and interesting people in the process. Here are some ways you and your company can help:

  • Posting interesting articles to the list, site, & social media
  • Contribute towards Online hosting costs (meetup.com, etc.)
  • Sponsoring food or drinks at meetup sessions - everyone likes food!

Thanks to everyone who attends and contributes to making OC DevOps a strong, thought-provoking group.
